SYSTEM OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This system manages an educational institution that combines:
- Daycare/Nursery (حضانة)
- Quran Classes (قرآن)
- Tutoring Groups (مجموعات تقوية)
- Literacy Classes (تعليم هجاء)
- Foundation Groups (مجموعات تأسيس)
- Courses (دورات)
- Summer Activities (نشاط صيفي)

================================================================================
                              LIST OF MODELS (65 TOTAL)
================================================================================

==================================
SECTION 1: STUDENT MANAGEMENT (6)
==================================

1. education.student (الطالب)
   - Main student record
   - Fields: name, code (auto), birthdate, photo, enrollment_date, period (morning/evening)
   - Links to: parent, class, department, bus, branch, enrollment_source
   - States: active, archived, pending_enrollment

2. education.parent (ولي الأمر)
   - Parent/Guardian information
   - Fields: name, job, phone, email, relation (father/mother/guardian)
   - Links to: student (One2many)

3. education.authorized.pickup (المصرح لهم بالاستلام)
   - People authorized to pick up student
   - Fields: name, relation, phone, id_number
   - Links to: student (Many2one)

4. education.sibling (الأخ/الأخت)
   - Sibling information for marketing purposes
   - Fields: name, birthdate, gender, age (computed)
   - Links to: student (Many2one)
   - Purpose: Notify when sibling reaches enrollment age (3.5 years)

5. education.student.note (ملاحظة الطالب)
   - Notes/observations on students
   - Fields: date, note, created_by
   - Links to: student (Many2one)

6. education.student.archive (أرشيف الطلاب)
   - Archive reason and history
   - Fields: archive_date, reason, notes
   - Links to: student (Many2one)


==================================
SECTION 2: ACADEMIC STRUCTURE (7)
==================================

7. education.department (القسم)
   - Main departments: Nursery, Quran, Tutoring, Literacy, Foundation, Courses, Summer
   - Fields: name, code, type, is_active
   - Links to: classes (One2many), levels (One2many), subjects (One2many)

8. education.level (المستوى)
   - Grade levels for tutoring (1st, 2nd, 3rd grade...)
   - Fields: name, sequence, department_id
   - Links to: department (Many2one)

9. education.class (الفصل)
   - Class/Group (Class 1, 2, 3... or teacher groups)
   - Fields: name, code, capacity, current_count (computed)
   - Links to: department (Many2one), teacher (Many2one), schedule (Many2one)

10. education.subject (المادة)
    - Subjects: Arabic, English, Math, Quran, Islamic Studies
    - Fields: name, code, department_id
    - Links to: department (Many2one)

11. education.schedule (الميعاد)
    - Time slots (11:00-12:00, 12:00-1:00, etc.)
    - Fields: name, time_from, time_to, period (morning/evening)

12. education.academic.year (العام الدراسي)
    - Academic year tracking
    - Fields: name, date_start, date_end, is_current

13. education.student.enrollment (تسجيل الطالب بالقسم)
    - Student enrollment in department (Many2Many with details)
    - Fields: student_id, department_id, class_id, subject_id, teacher_id, schedule_id
    - Purpose: Student can be enrolled in multiple departments simultaneously


==================================
SECTION 3: EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT (8)
==================================

14. education.employee (الموظف)
    - Teachers and staff
    - Fields: name, code, photo, birthdate, id_number, phone, email
    - Fields: qualification, university, graduation_date
    - Fields: job_title, category_id, salary, work_days_per_month
    - Fields: hire_date, branch_id, attendance_time_in, attendance_time_out
    - States: active, archived

15. education.employee.category (تصنيف الموظف)
    - Categories: Administration, Finance, Teaching Staff, Support
    - Fields: name, code

16. education.employee.qualification (مؤهل الموظف)
    - Qualifications and courses taken
    - Fields: name, institution, date, certificate
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)

17. education.employee.attendance (حضور الموظف)
    - Staff attendance records
    - Fields: date, time_in, time_out, status (present/absent/late)
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)

18. education.employee.salary (راتب الموظف)
    - Monthly salary records
    - Fields: month, year, basic_salary, deductions, bonuses, net_salary, paid
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)

19. education.employee.penalty (جزاء الموظف)
    - Disciplinary actions
    - Fields: date, reason, penalty_type, amount, notes
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)

20. education.employee.complaint (شكوى الموظف)
    - Complaints against employees
    - Fields: date, complaint, complainant, status, resolution
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)

21. education.employee.loan (سلفة الموظف)
    - Salary advances
    - Fields: date, amount, reason, status (pending/approved/paid/deducted)
    - Links to: employee (Many2one)


========================================
SECTION 4: SUBSCRIPTIONS & PAYMENTS (6)
========================================

22. education.subscription.type (نوع الاشتراك)
    - Subscription types: Nursery Fees, Quran Fees, Tutoring Fees, Bus Fees
    - Fields: name, code, amount, department_id
    - Links to: department (Many2one), payment_plan (Many2one)

23. education.payment.plan (نظام السداد)
    - Payment plans: Monthly, Annual, One-time
    - Fields: name, installment_count, interval_months
    - Example: Monthly = 12 installments, 1 month interval

24. education.student.subscription (اشتراك الطالب)
    - Student's subscription to a type
    - Fields: student_id, subscription_type_id, payment_plan_id
    - Fields: start_date, end_date, amount, status
    - Links to: student (Many2one), installments (One2many)

25. education.installment (الدفعة)
    - Individual installment (12 per year for monthly)
    - Fields: subscription_id, sequence, due_date, amount, is_paid, paid_date
    - Links to: subscription (Many2one), payments (One2many)

26. education.payment (السداد)
    - Actual payment transaction
    - Fields: installment_id, date, amount, payment_method, received_by
    - Links to: installment (Many2one), receipt (Many2one)

27. education.payment.receipt (إيصال السداد)
    - Payment receipt for printing
    - Fields: receipt_number (auto), date, student_id, amount, installments
    - Links to: student (Many2one), payments (One2many)


========================================
SECTION 5: EVALUATION & ASSESSMENT (7)
========================================

28. education.grade.scale (مقياس التقييم)
    - Grade scales types
    - Examples: "Yes/No", "Strong/Medium/Weak", "Excellent/Good/Acceptable"
    - Fields: name, code
    - Links to: values (One2many)

29. education.grade.scale.value (قيمة المقياس)
    - Individual values in a scale
    - Fields: name, scale_id, sequence, numeric_value
    - Examples: "Yes"=1, "No"=0 | "Excellent"=3, "Good"=2, "Acceptable"=1

30. education.evaluation.template (قالب التقييم)
    - Assessment template structure
    - Fields: name, code, subject_id, department_id
    - Links to: subject (Many2one), questions (One2many)
    - Note: Templates are uploaded as Excel files then parsed

31. education.evaluation.question (سؤال التقييم)
    - Questions/items in a template
    - Fields: template_id, sequence, question_text, grade_scale_id
    - Links to: template (Many2one), grade_scale (Many2one)

32. education.evaluation.goal (هدف التقييم)
    - Evaluation goals (shared or individual per student)
    - Fields: name, date, teacher_id, subject_id, class_id, template_id
    - Fields: is_shared_goal, goal_description
    - Links to: teacher (Many2one), students (Many2many)

33. education.student.evaluation (تقييم الطالب)
    - Student's actual evaluation record
    - Fields: student_id, goal_id, template_id, evaluator_id
    - Fields: date, summary_grade, notes, is_completed
    - Links to: student (Many2one), answers (One2many)

34. education.evaluation.answer (إجابة التقييم)
    - Student's answer/score per question
    - Fields: evaluation_id, question_id, grade_value_id, notes
    - Links to: evaluation (Many2one), question (Many2one)


==================================
SECTION 6: ATTENDANCE (2)
==================================

35. education.attendance (الحضور)
    - Daily attendance session
    - Fields: date, department_id, class_id, recorded_by
    - Links to: department (Many2one), class (Many2one), lines (One2many)

36. education.attendance.line (سطر الحضور)
    - Individual student attendance entry
    - Fields: attendance_id, student_id, status (present/absent), notes
    - Links to: attendance (Many2one), student (Many2one)
    - Default: All students marked present, mark absent ones

37. education.bus (الباص)

    - Fields: name, code, capacity, plate_number
    - Links to: driver (Many2one), routes (One2many)

38. education.driver (السائق)
    - Driver information
    - Fields: name, phone, license_number, photo
    - Links to: bus (Many2one)

39. education.bus.route (خط السير)
    - Bus routes

========================================
SECTION 8: TREASURY & ACCOUNTING (5)
========================================

41. education.treasury (الخزينة)
    - Daily treasury balance tracking
    - Fields: date, opening_balance, closing_balance, total_income, total_expense
    - Computed from transactions

42. education.transaction (حركة مالية)
    - Financial transaction (income/expense)
    - Fields: date, time, type (income/expense), category_id
    - Fields: amount, description, reference, created_by, branch_id
    - Links to: category (Many2one), branch (Many2one)
    - Note: Cannot be deleted once saved (mentioned as issue in recording)

43. education.transaction.category (تصنيف الحركة)
    - Transaction categories
    - Fields: name, code, type (income/expense)

44. education.income.type (نوع الإيراد)
    - Income types
    - Examples: Subscriptions, Book Sales, Registration Fees
    - Fields: name, code

45. education.expense.type (نوع المصروف)
    - Expense types
    - Examples: Salaries, Supplies, Utilities, Maintenance
    - Fields: name, code


==================================
SECTION 9: INVENTORY/BOOKS (5)
==================================

46. education.product.category (تصنيف المنتج)
    - Book categories
    - Examples: English Books, Arabic Books, Notebooks, Supplies
    - Fields: name, code

47. education.product (المنتج/الكتاب)
    - Books and products
    - Fields: name, code (auto), category_id, unit, purchase_price, sale_price
    - Fields: quantity_in, quantity_out, quantity_balance (computed)
    - Links to: category (Many2one), stock_in (One2many), stock_out (One2many)

48. education.stock.in (وارد المخزن)
    - Stock incoming (purchases)
    - Fields: date, product_id, quantity, unit_price, total_price
    - Links to: product (Many2one), lines (One2many)
    - Creates treasury transaction (expense)

49. education.stock.out (صادر المخزن)
    - Stock outgoing (sales)
    - Fields: date, product_id, quantity, unit_price, total_price
    - Links to: product (Many2one), lines (One2many)
    - Creates treasury transaction (income)

50. education.stock.move.line (سطر حركة المخزون)
    - Individual items in stock move
    - Fields: stock_move_id, product_id, quantity, unit_price
    - Links to: stock_in or stock_out (Many2one)

52. education.message.template (قالب الرسالة)

    - Fields: name, subject, body, trigger (manual/auto)

53. education.email.log (سجل الإيميلات)
    - Sent email history
    - Fields: date, recipient_email, subject, status (sent/failed), evaluation_id
    - Links to: student (Many2one), evaluation (Many2one)

59. education.settings (الإعدادات)

      * printer_name
      * payment_reminder_day (default: 10)

      * backup_path
      * email_address

60. education.user.permission (صلاحية المستخدم)

                              DASHBOARD FEATURES
================================================================================

MAIN DASHBOARD DISPLAYS:
------------------------
1. Total Students Count (active students in institution)
2. Today's Absences Count
3. Hosted Children Count (if applicable)
4. Current Treasury Balance
5. Charts:
   - Expenses breakdown by category
   - Income breakdown (subscriptions, book sales)
   - Monthly income vs expenses
6. Reminders list
7. Daily notes
8. Notifications (unpaid, absences, sibling alerts)
